Train Driving Experience

One normal day, like any other person, I walked to a train station. No, i did not go to take a train. Why does everyone keep asking me that? No one does that! My friend and I don't go to a train station to take a train. 2 people vs 7 billion! Do the math! Of course the majority of the world does what we do. Anyway, I kidnapped the train captain and drove the train. Normal so far, right? But, I accidentally drove off the tracks and rode down a hill into an airport. I hit a plane that was fueling and it exploded. Sadly no one was in the plane and no one got hurt.But on the bright side, there was another hillside on the other side of the airport, so i rode up on the hill side and flew off, hitting a plane in the air. My friend happened to have done the same thing as me, except he took a plane! What jolly news. Everyone died except for us because we used our passengers as body shields. Everyone in the airport died too because of the exploding fueling plane. Then quantum zombies spawned from the chemicals and explosions and killed the remaining survivors. I ate them all.
